About

FitTrack Edu is a platform that simplifies physical education management in schools.

Our app is designed to help teachers, students, and administrators organize sports activities, track performance, and manage documentation efficiently.

With FitTrack Edu, you can easily schedule events, manage student performance, organize school documents, and communicate with students and teachers.
